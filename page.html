Partnership with
Alumni Mentorship Program
With immense pleasure we want to announce that we are entering into partnership with Alumni Mentorship Program (AMP) IIT Roorkee.

This allows the first year students to not only get a mentor from the 3rd/4th/5th years but also get an alumni as their mentor. Thus each and every first year student gets a chance to interact with their seniors as well as alumni who can guide them in the long run, having themselves graduated.
This bridges the communication gap between alumni and first-year students by providing an environment for healthy discussion, guidance and one to one counseling. Not only first year students but 3rd/4th/5th year mentors will also gain from this arrangement by acquiring a platform for interactions with alumni.

Having had complementary plans, both SMP and AMP hope for a better interaction between freshers and alumni using SMP’s widespread presence on campus and AMP’s database and connections with the alumni.
